Mojito - Your Beach Vacation In A Glass

The Mojito is a rum based cocktail that is sweet, tangy and refreshing. Served chilled, it can refresh the thirstiest of days faster than any other beverage probably can. Mojitos are ideally made with mint, fresh lime juice, sugar, seltzer and (of course) rum. Served in a tall glass, with a whole lof of ice, they are a staple at nearly all watering holes in warmer climes.

One of the simplest recipes to make, Mojitos need no shaking, blending or layering. They need no fancy bar equipment. Just a tall glass (that you will also use to drink them in), a bar spoon and a bar measure is all you need to create this cocktail!

Ingredients

  • Rum (white or gold) - 60ml
  • Sugar (granulated) - 1tbsp
  • Lime Juice (fresh) - 15ml
  • Lime - 1 slice
  • Mint Leaves (fresh) - 10 to 15
  • Seltzer - 200ml
  • Ice cubes - 2 to 3 large cubes

Steps

  1. Put mint leaves in a tall collins glass
  2. Add rum
  3. Add sugar
  4. Stir ingredients with a bar spoon till the sugar is nearly dissolved
  5. Add the ice cubes
  6. Pour in seltzer slowly (to preserve fizz)
  7. Stir gently
  8. Garnish with a slice of lime
  9. Serve (and consume) chilled!

Cheers!
