Cosmopolitan

The Cosmopolitan - Classy As Cocktails Come!

The Cosmopolitan, or cosmo, is a sweet-citrus drink that is served chilled in a martini glass and has the capability to lift up your spirits as soon as you start sipping it.

Also called "Cosmo", this is a vodka based cocktail that's best consumed in the evenings, out from a cocktail glass, and at bars where you have enough elbow room to not have it spilled!

Ingredients

  • Vodka (traditionally, a lemon vodka is used, but a normal vodka will work just as well) - 45ml
  • Triple-sec - 15ml
  • Cranberry juice - 60ml
  • Lime juice - 10ml
  • Ice-cubes - 2 or 3
  • Orange zest - small peel

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients except the orange zest into the shaker.
  2. Shake well, and pour it through a strainer into the martini glass.
  3. To finish the drink, take the orange zest, squeeze the oils onto a lit flame on top, and slide the spent zest into the drink.

And as easy as that, you have your Cosmopolitan ready to drink!
